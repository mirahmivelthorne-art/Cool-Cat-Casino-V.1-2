Chapter One

A Casino and Nine Lives

Uncle Whiskers built the Cool Cat Casino as a classy little place where cats could relax, make friends, and—if fortune purred—win a few Catnip Coins.

After his passing, the keys found their way to his nephew, Onyx.

🐾

Dear Onyx,

If you're reading this, I've finally used up the last of my nine lives. Don't look so glum. I spent them well.

This casino was never just about coins. It was a warm chair on a rainy night. A good story at the bar. The thrill of maybe, just maybe, hitting three sardines in a row.

I'm leaving it to you because you have good instincts, a steady paw, and Benny won't stop pestering you until the old machines work again.

“Run it, but don't run it into the ground.”

— Uncle Whiskers
